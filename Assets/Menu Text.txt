Instructions:
- Drag and drop the buildings and streets onto the grid in the middle
- Puzzle pieces do not rotate. If there's a hole in your puzzle, rearrange it
- Move the camera by holding down the right mouse button

- Puzzle Size controls the size of the *next* puzzle.
- Piece Size controls the size of the pieces for the *next* puzzle
- Adjust the music and sound effects volume to your taste.
- Play starts a new puzzle
- Quit exits the game
- The Menu button (in the top right) shows and hides all of these handy hints
